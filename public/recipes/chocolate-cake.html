Chocolate Cake

Rich bakery-style chocolate cake with deep cocoa flavor, soft moist layers, silky texture, and luxurious homemade taste. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, and premium dessert lovers.

Prep Time

25 mins

Bake Time

35 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • ¾ cup (75g) cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee or hot water

Chocolate Frosting

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup melted chocolate
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp milk

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two cake pans.

2

Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together.

3

Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.

4

Slowly pour in hot coffee or hot water to enhance chocolate flavor.

5

Divide batter evenly into cake pans.

6

Bake for 30–35 minutes until soft and fully cooked.

7

Cool cake layers completely before frosting.

8

For frosting, beat butter until creamy then add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, melted chocolate, vanilla, and milk.

9

Layer and frost cake beautifully for premium bakery presentation.

Tips

  • Use hot coffee for richer chocolate flavor.
  • Do not overmix batter to keep cake soft.
  • Chill frosting slightly before decorating.
  • Store in airtight container for freshness.
