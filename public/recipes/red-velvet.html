Red Velvet Cake

Classic moist red velvet cake with smooth cream cheese frosting.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Bake Time: 35 minutes
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Servings: 10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups (315g) flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Mix wet ingredients separately.
  4. Combine both mixtures.
  5. Pour into cake pans.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes.
  7. Cool completely.
  8. Frost with cream cheese frosting.

Tips

  • Use gel food coloring for vibrant red color.
  • Do not overmix batter.
  • Cool before frosting.
