Red Velvet Cake

Luxurious bakery-style red velvet cake with soft moist layers, rich cocoa flavor, vibrant red color, and creamy smooth frosting. Perfect for celebrations, birthdays, and premium dessert lovers.

Prep Time

25 mins

Bake Time

35 mins

Difficulty

Intermediate

Servings

10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups (300g) sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 225g cream cheese
  • ½ cup (115g) butter
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two cake pans with parchment paper.

2

Whisk flour, baking soda, cocoa powder, and salt together in a bowl.

3

In another bowl, mix sugar, eggs, oil, buttermilk, food coloring, vanilla extract, and vinegar.

4

Gradually combine dry ingredients into wet mixture until smooth and silky.

5

Pour batter evenly into cake pans and smooth the tops.

6

Bake for 32–35 minutes until cake layers are soft and fully cooked.

7

Allow cake layers to cool completely before frosting.

8

For frosting, beat cream cheese and butter until creamy, then mix in powdered sugar and vanilla.

9

Layer cake with cream cheese frosting and decorate beautifully.

Tips

  • Use gel food coloring for vibrant red color.
  • Do not overmix batter to keep cake soft.
  • Chill frosting before decorating for cleaner layers.
  • Store refrigerated for best freshness.
