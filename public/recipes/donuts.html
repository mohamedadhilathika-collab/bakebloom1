Chocolate Donuts

Soft fluffy donuts coated with rich homemade chocolate glaze.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Bake Time: 15 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Servings: 12 donuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) flour
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Chocolate Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Mix wet ingredients separately.
  4. Combine mixtures into smooth batter.
  5. Fill donut tray.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes.
  7. Cool donuts completely.
  8. Dip in chocolate glaze.

Tips

  • Do not overfill donut molds.
  • Cool before glazing.
  • Add sprinkles for bakery-style look.
