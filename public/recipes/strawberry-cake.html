Strawberry Cake

Soft bakery-style strawberry cake layered with rich strawberry frosting, fresh berry flavor, fluffy texture, and luxurious homemade sweetness. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, and premium dessert lovers.

Prep Time

25 mins

Bake Time

35 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 1¾ cups (350g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strawberry puree
  • ½ cup milk

Strawberry Frosting

  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup strawberry puree
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two cake pans.

2

Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together.

3

Beat butter and sugar until fluffy and creamy.

4

Add eggs one at a time and mix well. Stir in vanilla extract.

5

Mix strawberry puree and milk into the batter gradually.

6

Pour batter evenly into cake pans.

7

Bake for 32–35 minutes until soft and fluffy.

8

Cool cake layers completely before frosting.

9

Beat frosting ingredients until smooth and creamy.

10

Decorate with fresh strawberries for premium bakery presentation.

Tips

  • Use fresh strawberry puree for best flavor.
  • Do not overmix batter to keep cake soft.
  • Chill frosting slightly before decorating.
  • Add fresh strawberries on top for premium presentation.
