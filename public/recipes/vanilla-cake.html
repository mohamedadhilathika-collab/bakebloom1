Vanilla Cake

Soft and fluffy bakery-style vanilla cake with rich buttery flavor.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Bake Time: 35 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Servings: 8 slices

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease and line cake pan.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time.
  6. Add vanilla extract.
  7. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk.
  8. Pour batter into cake pan.
  9. Bake for 35 minutes.
  10. Cool before frosting.

Tips

  • Use room-temperature ingredients.
  • Do not overmix the batter.
  • Cool cake fully before decorating.
