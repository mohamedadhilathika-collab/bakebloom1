Classic Vanilla Cake

A soft, fluffy, bakery-style vanilla cake with rich buttery flavor, smooth crumb texture, and luxurious homemade taste. This beginner-friendly cake is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, tea time, and premium desserts.

Prep Time

20 mins

Bake Time

35 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

8 slices

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 2½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1¾ cups (350g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) sour cream

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two round cake pans with parchment paper.

2

In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt until evenly combined.

3

In another large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light, creamy, and fluffy.

4

Add eggs one at a time while mixing continuously. Stir in vanilla extract.

5

Add dry ingredients gradually along with milk and sour cream. Mix until smooth.

6

Pour batter evenly into prepared cake pans and smooth the tops.

7

Bake for 32–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

8

Allow cakes to cool fully before frosting or serving for best bakery-style texture.

Tips

  • Use room-temperature butter and eggs for smoother batter.
  • Do not overmix the batter to keep the cake soft and fluffy.
  • Add vanilla bean paste for richer flavor.
  • Chill cake layers before frosting for clean decoration.
