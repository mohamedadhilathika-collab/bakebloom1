Chocolate Donuts

Soft fluffy bakery-style chocolate donuts coated with glossy rich chocolate glaze, light airy texture, and luxurious homemade flavor. Perfect for dessert lovers, tea-time snacks, and beginner bakers.

Prep Time

25 mins

Bake Time

15 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

12 donuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (100g) sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Chocolate Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease donut tray lightly.

2

Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.

3

In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.

4

Gradually combine wet and dry ingredients into smooth donut batter.

5

Transfer batter into piping bag and fill donut molds about ¾ full.

6

Bake for 12–15 minutes until donuts become soft and lightly golden.

7

Allow donuts to cool completely before adding glaze.

8

For glaze, whisk powdered sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and vanilla extract until glossy.

9

Dip donuts into chocolate glaze and decorate with sprinkles if desired.

Tips

  • Do not overmix batter to keep donuts soft and airy.
  • Use piping bag for cleaner donut shapes.
  • Cool donuts fully before glazing.
  • Add chocolate sprinkles for bakery-style presentation.
