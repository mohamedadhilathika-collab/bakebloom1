Butter Cookies

Classic crispy butter cookies with rich homemade bakery flavor.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 12 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Servings: 20 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Beat butter and sugar together.
  3. Add egg and vanilla.
  4. Add flour and salt.
  5. Mix into soft dough.
  6. Shape cookies on baking tray.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
  8. Cool before serving.

Tips

  • Chill dough for 20 minutes for crisp cookies.
  • Do not overbake.
  • Store in airtight container.
