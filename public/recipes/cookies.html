Butter Cookies

Classic crispy butter cookies with rich homemade bakery flavor, golden edges, soft buttery texture, and luxurious melt-in-mouth taste. Perfect for tea time, celebrations, and beginner bakers.

Prep Time

15 mins

Bake Time

12 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

20 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp milk if needed

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.

2

Beat softened butter and sugar together until light, creamy, and fluffy.

3

Add egg and vanilla extract, then mix until smooth and fully combined.

4

Gradually add flour and salt. Mix gently until a soft dough forms.

5

If dough feels dry, add 1–2 tablespoons of milk for softer texture.

6

Shape cookies and place them evenly on the baking tray.

7

Bake for 10–12 minutes until edges turn lightly golden brown.

8

Allow cookies to cool completely for crispy bakery-style texture.

Tips

  • Use room-temperature butter for smooth dough.
  • Do not overbake to keep cookies soft inside.
  • Chill dough for 15 minutes for cleaner cookie shapes.
  • Store in airtight container for freshness.
