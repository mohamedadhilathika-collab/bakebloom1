Chocolate Brownies

Rich, fudgy homemade brownies with a soft gooey center. Perfect for beginner bakers.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 30 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Servings: 9 brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) cocoa powder
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease a square baking pan.
  3. Melt butter in a bowl.
  4. Add sugar and mix well.
  5. Add eggs one at a time.
  6. Mix in vanilla extract.
  7. Add cocoa powder, flour, and salt.
  8. Fold in chocolate chips.
  9. Pour batter into baking pan.
  10. Bake for 28–30 minutes.
  11. Cool completely before slicing.

Tips

  • Do not overbake for fudgy texture.
  • Use dark chocolate chips for richer flavor.
  • Cool fully before cutting.
