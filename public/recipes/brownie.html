Chocolate Brownies

Rich, luxurious bakery-style chocolate brownies with a deep cocoa flavor, crispy crackly top, soft gooey center, and intense chocolate texture. Perfect for beginner bakers who want premium homemade desserts.

Prep Time

15 mins

Bake Time

30 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

9 brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) cocoa powder
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a square baking pan with parchment paper.

2

Melt butter completely in a bowl. Add sugar and whisk until glossy and smooth.

3

Add eggs one at a time while mixing continuously. Stir in vanilla extract.

4

Fold cocoa powder, flour, and salt gently into the mixture until fully combined.

5

Add chocolate chips for extra gooey chocolate texture.

6

Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake for 28–30 minutes.

7

Allow brownies to cool completely before slicing for clean bakery-style squares.
