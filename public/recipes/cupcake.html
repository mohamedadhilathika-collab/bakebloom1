Vanilla Cupcakes

Soft fluffy bakery-style vanilla cupcakes topped with creamy smooth frosting, rich buttery flavor, and luxurious texture. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, and beginner bakers who love premium desserts.

Prep Time

20 mins

Bake Time

18 mins

Difficulty

Beginner

Servings

12 cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup (120ml) whole milk

Frosting Ingredients

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line cupcake tray with paper liners.

2

Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a mixing bowl.

3

Beat butter and sugar until light, fluffy, and creamy.

4

Add eggs one at a time while mixing continuously. Stir in vanilla extract.

5

Gradually mix dry ingredients and milk into the batter until smooth.

6

Fill cupcake liners about ¾ full for perfect bakery-style rise.

7

Bake for 18 minutes until cupcakes become soft and lightly golden.

8

Allow cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

9

For frosting, beat butter until creamy. Add powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract.

10

Pipe frosting beautifully onto cupcakes for premium bakery presentation.

Tips

  • Use room-temperature butter and eggs for smoother batter.
  • Do not overmix batter to keep cupcakes soft and fluffy.
  • Cool cupcakes fully before frosting.
  • Use piping bags for bakery-style decoration.
