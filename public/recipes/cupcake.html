Vanilla Cupcakes

Soft and fluffy vanilla cupcakes with smooth creamy frosting. Perfect for beginner bakers.

Preparation Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Bake Time: 18 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Servings: 12 cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Line cupcake tray with paper liners.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time.
  6. Mix in vanilla extract.
  7. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk.
  8. Fill cupcake liners 3/4 full.
  9. Bake for 18 minutes.
  10. Cool before frosting.

Frosting Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk

Frosting Instructions

  1. Beat butter until creamy.
  2. Add powdered sugar slowly.
  3. Add vanilla and milk.
  4. Pipe frosting onto cupcakes.

Tips

  • Do not overmix batter.
  • Cool cupcakes before frosting.
  • Use piping bag for bakery-style look.
